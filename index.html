Virat Kohli
Bio

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and one of the leading batsmen in the world.

Full Name: Virat Kohli
Date of Birth: November 5, 1988
Place of Birth: Delhi, India

Early Life:

Virat Kohli was born in Delhi to a Punjabi family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer, and his mother, Saroj Kohli, is a housewife. Kohli showed early promise in cricket and began playing at a young age. His passion and talent for the sport led him to join the West Delhi Cricket Academy when he was just nine years old.

International Debut:

Kohli made his international debut for India in an ODI against Sri Lanka in August 2008. He made his Test debut in 2011 against West Indies. Over the years, Kohli established himself as a key player in the Indian cricket team.

Personal Life:

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. The couple tied the knot in December 2017. They have a daughter named Vamika, born in January 2021.
